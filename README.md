# 🛒 Walmart Sales Dashboard

A comprehensive Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, product trends, customer purchasing behavior, and regional sales performance using Walmart sales data.

Built using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Timelines, and Excel Dashboarding techniques to provide actionable business insights.

---

## 📌 Project Highlights

💰 **$725,458 Total Revenue**
📈 **$108,418 Total Profit**
🛒 **3,203 Total Orders**
💵 **$226.49 Average Order Value**
🏆 **Top Category:** Chairs
🏅 **Top Product:** Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Executive KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Top Category
* Top Product

### Sales Analysis

* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Monthly Profit Trend Analysis
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Sales by Category
* Profit by Category
* Top 10 Cities by Sales

### Interactive Filters

* Order Date Timeline
* Category Slicer
* City Slicer

### Key Business Insights

* Identifies best-selling products
* Highlights highest revenue-generating categories
* Shows top-performing cities
* Detects seasonal sales patterns
* Tracks profit performance across categories

## 📎 Business Use Case

Designed for retail businesses and sales teams to:

* Monitor overall sales performance
* Identify top-selling products
* Analyze category-wise profitability
* Compare sales across different cities
* Discover seasonal trends and opportunities
* Support data-driven business decisions

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Chairs generated the highest revenue among all categories.
* Los Angeles contributed the highest sales revenue.
* December recorded the highest monthly sales.
* A small number of products generated a large portion of total revenue.
* Profit trends show consistent business growth throughout the year.
